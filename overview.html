Platform Overview

One platform. Every corner of your services business.

MB PSA connects all the moving parts of a professional services organisation — from winning new clients to delivering projects, tracking time, managing people, and closing invoices. Everything speaks to everything else.

What each pillar does for your business

Plain-language explanations — no jargon, no tech-speak.

📋
Portfolio & Delivery
Projects · Milestones · Delivery

Think of this as your master control room for every client project. It shows what's being worked on, who's responsible, and whether things are running on time and on budget — all in one place.

  • Track every project from kick-off to completion
  • See milestones, deadlines and delivery status at a glance
  • Spot projects that are at risk before they go off the rails
  • Manage multiple projects and programmes simultaneously
  • Give clients transparent progress updates automatically
⏱
Time & Resources
Timesheets · Allocation · Capacity

Knowing where your people's time goes is the heartbeat of any services firm. This pillar tracks hours, matches the right people to the right work, and prevents your best talent from being over-committed or sitting idle.

  • Simple mobile-friendly timesheets staff actually complete
  • See who's available and who's fully booked in real time
  • Match skills and seniority to project requirements
  • Manage leave, public holidays and part-time contracts
  • Forecast future capacity so you can plan ahead confidently
💰
Finance & Billing
Invoicing · Revenue · Margins

Turn the work your team has done directly into accurate invoices — no manual spreadsheets, no hours lost chasing timesheet data. Keep a clear picture of revenue, costs and profitability across every engagement.

  • Auto-generate invoices from approved timesheets and expenses
  • Support fixed-fee, time & materials, and milestone billing
  • Track revenue recognised vs. revenue invoiced
  • Monitor project margins and flag when costs run over
  • Sync with accounting systems (Xero, Sage, QuickBooks)
🤝
Client Management
CRM · Relationships · Pipeline

Everything about your clients — contacts, history, active work, upcoming opportunities — lives in one connected place. So whoever is speaking to a client is always up to speed, and no relationship ever slips through the cracks.

  • Full view of every client: contacts, contracts and history
  • Track sales pipeline from first conversation to signed deal
  • Link opportunities directly to delivered projects
  • Log calls, emails and meeting notes in one place
  • Identify upsell and renewal opportunities early
📊
Reporting & Insights
Dashboards · Status · Analytics

Stop spending Friday afternoons building status reports in PowerPoint. MB PSA generates live dashboards and automated reports — for clients, for your leadership team, and for the people doing the work.

  • Weekly status reports created automatically from live data
  • Executive dashboards for leadership with KPIs at a glance
  • Client-facing portals showing project health transparently
  • Drill down from summary view to individual tasks and hours
  • Export to PDF or share via secure link
👥
People & HR
Leave · Expenses · Wellbeing

Your consultants and staff are your most valuable asset. This pillar handles the day-to-day people operations — leave requests, expense claims, performance check-ins — so managers can focus on leading rather than administrating.

  • Leave requests approved in seconds, calendars updated instantly
  • Expense claims submitted from a phone, approved in the platform
  • Track utilisation and flag when people are burning out
  • Personal dashboards for each team member to see their work
  • Onboarding checklists for new starters
🤖
AI & Automation
Agents · Workflows · Intelligence

MB PSA uses AI to do the repetitive work — so your team doesn't have to. From chasing overdue timesheets to surfacing project risks before they become problems, automation keeps the business running smoothly in the background.

  • AI agents nudge staff to submit timesheets automatically
  • Smart risk scoring flags projects likely to overrun
  • Automated approval workflows (expenses, leave, invoices)
  • Natural language Q&A: "How are we tracking against budget?"
  • AI-assisted proposal writing and scope estimation
